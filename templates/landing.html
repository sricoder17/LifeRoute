How It Works

One request. The whole network responds.

01
Request anything, in one go
Blood units, ventilators, specialists, or antidotes — bundle multiple items into a single cart-style request.
02
Smart, traffic-aware matching
The engine tries to fill your whole request from one hospital first; if not, it intelligently splits it across the network — always picking the fastest real route, not just the nearest one.
03
Real roads, live traffic, live ETA
Every shipment follows an actual road route with live traffic conditions factored into arrival time — tracked in real time on the network map.
04
Emergency SOS broadcast
One tap sends a Tier-1 alert to the entire network, with optional automated voice calling to an on-call number.
